Hot Water
Description

Hot water is yet another form of water. This form of water is a more soothing and calming form of water than cold water. Be careful not to consume water that is too hot, or you will burn your tongue and experience relatively mild pain.

Ingredients:
  • Water
  • Source of heat, usually stovetop w/ pot or water boiler
  • Aesthetic mug
Steps:
  1. Obtain water boiler
  2. Fill water boiler with water
  3. Heat water
  4. Locate mug
  5. Fill mug with hot water
  6. Wait for hot water to cool down
  7. Drink hot water
