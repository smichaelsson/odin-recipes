Water

Description

Water is a very important and crucial thing for human beings. You can find it in the mountains and harvest it in large quantities to supply a city. Drink water, roughly 120oz of it a day or something ridiculous like that. It'll make your brain and body heppy.

Ingredients:

  • Water
  • Aesthetic glass cup

Steps:

  1. Grab glass cup
  2. Position near stream of water
  3. Capture stream of water so water flows into cup
  4. Be mindful of when water nears top of cup
  5. Quickly but not smoothly move cup away from stream
  6. Smell the freshness of the water
  7. Position cup near lips, prepare to consume life sustenance
  8. Drink water
