Cold Water
Description

Cold water is a refreshing and sometimes invigorating form of water. It contributes to a completely different experience of drinking water. Try drinking this form of water when you need a small kick of energy on your path to successfully consume 120+oz of water a day.

Ingredients:
  • Water
  • Ice or Refrigerator
  • Aesthetic glass cup
Steps:
  1. Obtain ice or locate refrigerator
  2. Locate glass cup
  3. Fill glass cup with ice, or fill glass cup with water if using refrigerator
  4. Fill glass that has ice with water, or wait ~2+ hours for water in refrigerator
  5. Drink cold water
